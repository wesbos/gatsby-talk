Tips

Everything must go through Gatsby.

One of the biggest Hurdles is thinking in terms of gatsby.

Where do I put my images?

You don't. You import them into your template. This way Gatsby is aware of them and can compress them

Where do I link stylesheets?

You don't. You import them into your template. This way Gatsby can compute critical css.

Where do I fetch data?

You source the data into Gatsby first. This way you can statically render it, and re-render only the templates needed because Gatsby knows where that data was used.

You get the point

If Gatsby is aware of every single image, asset, CSS style and node of data, it's able to do all the amazing things we talked about earlier.
